Parchment 2: A Simple Web Framework

Minimalistic and responsive

Parchment2

Parchment2 is a rebuild of the original Parchment theme. Parchment was designed to have a modern and responsive theme. Parchment2 is designed to continue that goal, but meeting the latest in ever-evolving design standards.

About the design

Parchment2 has been heavily influenced by Microsoft's Fluent Design System. Other design considerations came from the importance of mobile support. A consistent experience was targeted. Parchment featured numerous settings for different screen sizes. Parchment2 has significantly fewer, as it is designed to meet requirements for all screen sizes.
